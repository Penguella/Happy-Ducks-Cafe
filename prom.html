How do promotions work and why you need an account?

The reason you need an account is to be able to track your progress towards the goal - as well for us to make sure there are no mistakes when it comes to calculating the score- . For every 5 lei spent on our products you receive a point. Collect at least 20 points and you'll receive a plushie of our duck mascot. An event usually lasts 1 month and since you can team up with friends ( this is where the reservation via account comes handy) there should be no problem reaching the goal.

Can you save progress from last event?

No. Sadly, the progress bar will be reset at the end of the event.

How will I see my progress?

We'll update the page to show a progress bar and, once we have a database, you'll be able to see it right here
